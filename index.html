Our Story

Founded in 2019, Smart Klean started as a small neighborhood laundry service with a simple mission: to provide exceptional laundry care with convenience and reliability.

What began as a single location has grown into a trusted name in laundry services across the city, thanks to our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

We're proud to use eco-friendly detergents and energy-efficient equipment, reducing our environmental impact while delivering outstanding results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

We currently serve the entire metro area including downtown and all surrounding neighborhoods. During the booking process, simply enter your address to confirm we service your location.

Simply place your clothes in any bag (we'll return them in our reusable Smart Klean bag). There's no need to sort - we'll handle that for you. Please:

  • Empty all pockets
  • Remove any non-laundry items
  • Note any special stains or instructions

We use premium, eco-friendly detergents that are tough on stains but gentle on fabrics and the environment. All our detergents are hypoallergenic and free from harsh chemicals. If you have specific detergent preferences or allergies, please let us know in the special instructions when booking.

No problem! You can specify a safe place for us to leave or pick up your laundry (porch, doorman, etc.). For security, we recommend open and clear instructions from our customers so we can deliver to precise location and to the right person.

We stand behind our work 100%. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our service, let us know within 24 hours and we'll either re-clean the items for free. Terms and Conditions apply.
